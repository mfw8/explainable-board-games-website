Explainable Board Games

Making AI-driven board game strategies transparent and understandable

  • Vision

Vision

Our project aims to bridge the gap between artificial intelligence and human understanding in the context of board games. While AI systems have achieved superhuman performance in games like Chess, Go, and other complex board games, their decision-making processes often remain opaque. We are developing a system that not only plays board games at a high level but also provides clear, intuitive explanations for its strategic choices.

By combining modern AI techniques with explainable AI (XAI) methods, we create an interactive learning environment where players can understand the reasoning behind optimal moves. This project has applications in education, game design, and AI transparency research. Our goal is to demonstrate that powerful AI systems can be both effective and interpretable, making them valuable tools for learning and strategic thinking.

Through this work, we hope to contribute to the broader conversation about AI interpretability and show how transparent AI systems can enhance human decision-making rather than simply replace it.
